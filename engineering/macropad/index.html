macropad

The macropad v1 is a project I began to develop in the winter of 2015 for a high school science fair. I continued with the macropad v2 in 2016 at a summer engineering program, and am currently working on the macropad v3. The final product may be patented, so unfortunately I am unable to share specific information about the device on this site. If you are interested in the macropad project, feel free to contact me as I may be able to release more information in the future.
